Construction Supervisor

Learn the skills needed to lead and manage construction projects effectively.

About the Course

Our Construction Supervisor Training is a one-year comprehensive program designed to equip students with the skills and knowledge required to manage and supervise construction projects efficiently.

The course blends theoretical knowledge with hands-on experience, making it perfect for individuals aiming to become professional and reliable site supervisors in the booming construction industry.

What You'll Learn

  • Basics of Civil Engineering and Construction Materials
  • Site Safety Regulations and Labor Management
  • Construction Planning and Cost Estimation
